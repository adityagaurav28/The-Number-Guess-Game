RULES
  • A random number will be generated in the range of 1 to 100(both inclusive).

SCORING
  • If you guess the correct number then you will be 200 points.
  • You will get 100 points for each chance left.
  • If you guess the correct number then your final score will be the sum of the above two scores.
  • If you fail to guess the correct number then your final score will be 100 divided by the minimum difference between the original number and the guessed number.
